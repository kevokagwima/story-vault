About Story Vault

Inspired by my love for storytelling and the desire to create a platform where everyone can share their narratives, I embarked on this journey in early 2025. The idea blossomed during a personal experience where I found solace in sharing my own stories with friends. This project is not just a coding assignment; it's a passion project that aims to foster community and connection.
